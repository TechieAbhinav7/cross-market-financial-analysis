## Overview

Instead of exclusively consider either the macroeconomic variable or financial performance of the market, the paper aims at jointly considering the influence on both macro variable, systemic risk, downside risk, and the performance of market etc. Investors consider a global equity market including the US, Germany, India for capital investment while also accounting for issues like the economic progress, exchange rate movement, market risk, etc. This study has addressed the impact on international equity market returns under un-hedged currency risk exposure and will compare between developed market and emerging market as well by considering macroeconomic conditions and the impact on financial market risks. So rather than focusing only on macroeconomics variables or only on the financial performance of markets individually, the paper will tries to address both with a holistic view incorporating macroeconomic variable, systemic risk, downside risk, market performance, etc. In a single analysis covering a period between 2010-2024 using unbalanced panel data. 

Methods Value at Risk (VaR), Sharpe ratio, Treynor Ratio, Jensen’s Alpha and Sortino Ratio along with the examination of GDP growth trend, estimation of risk.
